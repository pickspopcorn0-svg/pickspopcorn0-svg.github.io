Editorial • Donghua

Our Favorite Donghua

Donghua is not just animation. It is modern fantasy told through ancient mythology, cultivation, and the idea that power is something you earn through suffering and discipline. These stories take time. They reward patience. And when they hit, they feel massive.

These four series represent what Donghua does best: long journeys, deep worlds, and characters who are shaped by hardship rather than shortcuts.

Soul Land

Cultivation • Brotherhood • Long-form fantasy

Soul Land is the series that made many people fall in love with Donghua. It is not about one hero becoming powerful. It is about a group of people growing together over time.

You watch these characters from their youth into adulthood. Their friendships, rivalries, and losses all build naturally. When something tragic happens, it hurts because you have lived with them.

Very few animated series create this sense of emotional history. Soul Land does it better than almost any.

Battle Through the Heavens

Underdog • Revenge • Power fantasy

This is one of the most satisfying rise-from-nothing stories in Donghua.

The hero starts as someone everyone looks down on. Every step forward is earned through humiliation, pain, and relentless training. There are no easy victories.

What makes it addictive is the feeling of momentum. Every arc pushes him higher. Every enemy is stronger. And you want to see how far he can go.

Perfect World

Gods • Ancient clans • Epic fantasy

Perfect World feels like watching legends unfold.

The scale is enormous. Ancient bloodlines, divine beasts, forgotten realms, and battles that feel bigger than history itself.

This is Donghua at its most cinematic. When it hits, it feels like myth brought to life.

Shrouding the Heavens

Mystery • Cultivation • Cosmic fantasy

This is the most atmospheric of the four.

Shrouding the Heavens moves slowly, but deliberately. It feels like reading an ancient novel, where every discovery reveals something larger about the universe.

If you enjoy deep lore, mystery, and cosmic scale, this is essential Donghua.
