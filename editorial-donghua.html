Editorial • Donghua

My Favorite Donghua

Donghua is not just animation. It is epic storytelling built on cultivation, destiny, ancient bloodlines, and the slow, painful climb from weakness to power. These stories reward patience — and when they hit, they feel larger than life.

These four series represent the soul of modern Donghua: long journeys, deep worlds, and characters who grow through suffering, discipline, and will.

Popcorn Picks • Curated Fantasy

1) Soul Land

Cultivation • Friendship • Destiny

Soul Land is one of the few long-form Donghua that truly earns its emotional weight. You do not just watch characters grow — you grow with them.

The bond between the team, the slow power progression, and the feeling of moving through different eras of their lives makes this feel like a fantasy epic.

If Donghua has a gateway masterpiece, this is it.

2) Battle Through the Heavens

Underdog • Revenge • Power

This is one of the most satisfying rise-from-nothing stories in Donghua.

Every humiliation, every loss, and every breakthrough feels personal. It turns anger and ambition into fuel.

Pure power-fantasy done right.

3) Perfect World

Gods • Monsters • Ancient realms

Perfect World is Donghua at its most mythic.

It feels like watching legends being written — huge battles, ancient clans, and a hero constantly tested by fate.

This is epic fantasy in motion.

4) Shrouding the Heavens

Mystery • Cultivation • Ancient secrets

This is the quiet, atmospheric side of Donghua.

The story moves like an ancient novel — slow, heavy, mysterious, and powerful.

If you love deep lore and cosmic scale, this is essential.
