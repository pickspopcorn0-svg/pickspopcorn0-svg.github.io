Best Chinese Fantasy Dramas

Chinese fantasy dramas include xianxia (immortals, gods, reincarnation), wuxia fantasy (martial world, sects, honor), and cultivation stories (power gained through training). These shows are usually long and character-driven, so the story has time to build relationships, rivalries, and consequences.

Listening Snow Tower

2019 · 56 Episodes
Wuxia FantasyRomanceMartial Arts

Set in the martial world, the story follows Xiao Yiqing, the leader of the Listening Snow Tower, an organization known for dealing with dangerous conflicts between sects. Because of its position, the Tower is constantly surrounded by enemies, political pressure, and hidden threats.

Shu Jingrong becomes involved with the Tower while carrying her own past tied to revenge and old grudges. As rival forces close in, both characters are pulled into shifting alliances and power struggles where trust is hard to keep.

Main cast: Qin Junjie, Yuan Bingyan

Ancient Love Poetry

2021 · 49 Episodes
XianxiaGodsReincarnation

In a world of ancient gods and immortal realms, Shang Gu grows from an inexperienced true god into someone forced to carry heavy responsibilities. Her relationship with Bai Jue is tested by divine duty, long separations, and the way major choices can echo across centuries.

The drama focuses on fate, sacrifice, and love that survives changing identities and time, with a myth-scale setting full of celestial rules and old conflicts.

Main cast: Zhou Dongyu, Xu Kai, Liu Xueyi

Who Rules the World

2022 · 40 Episodes
WuxiaPoliticsHidden Identity

Famous in the martial world, Bai Fengxi and Hei Fengxi cross paths as rivals and allies while hiding real identities connected to political power. Their partnership grows through shared battles and strategy rather than sudden fate.

The story balances martial arts with court politics, focusing on intelligence, leadership, and loyalty as different factions compete for control.

Main cast: Yang Yang, Zhao Lusi

Love and Redemption

2020 · 59 Episodes
XianxiaFatePast Lives

Xuan Ji is born without normal emotions, while Si Feng carries secrets and suffering linked to his origin. Their bond is shaped by fate, past lives, and repeated trials that push their relationship through misunderstandings, sacrifice, and painful choices.

This is a high-drama xianxia built around long suffering romance and karmic consequences across lifetimes.

Main cast: Yuan Bingyan, Cheng Yi

Ashes of Love

2018 · 63 Episodes
XianxiaImmortalsRomance

Jin Mi grows up sheltered from complicated emotions and becomes entangled with two powerful immortal figures, where love, jealousy, and misunderstandings slowly turn the story darker. As conflicts between realms intensify, choices made in anger or pride become difficult to undo.

Main cast: Yang Zi, Deng Lun, Luo Yunxi

Battle Through the Heavens

2018 · 45 Episodes
CultivationMartial FantasyGrowth

Xiao Yan loses his talent and status and must rebuild himself in a cultivation world where strength is earned through training, experience, and survival. The story focuses on steady growth, rivalries, and becoming powerful enough to protect what matters.

Main cast: Leo Wu (Wu Lei)

The Untamed

2019 · 50 Episodes
XianxiaDark FantasyCultivation

Wei Wuxian follows a cultivation path that the orthodox world fears and condemns. After major tragedy and a long absence, his return brings buried truths back to the surface, forcing old allies and enemies to confront what really happened and who benefited from it.

The story blends mystery, loyalty, and moral conflict in a world where “righteousness” is often controlled by power.

Main cast: Xiao Zhan, Wang Yibo
