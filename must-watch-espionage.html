EDITORIAL • ESPIONAGE

Must-Watch Espionage TV Shows

A hand-picked collection of serious, realistic spy dramas.

Popcorn Picks • Updated 2026

I’ve always been drawn to espionage stories that feel grounded and uncomfortable rather than flashy. Not slick agents with gadgets, but people living double lives and slowly losing who they really are.

These five series live in that space. They focus on tradecraft, psychology, and the emotional cost of intelligence work.

1. Le Bureau des Légendes (The Bureau)

France • 2015–2020 • 5 Seasons

The most realistic spy series ever made. Undercover agents live false lives for years, until they forget who they really are.

Quiet, devastating, and brutally authentic.

2. Slow Horses

UK • 2022– • 4+ Seasons

Disgraced MI5 agents uncover secrets no one else sees. Dry humor mixed with ruthless realism.

3. The Spy

2019 • True Story

The true story of an agent who lived inside the enemy for years — alone, trapped, and slowly breaking.

4. Berlin Station

2016–2019 • CIA Europe

Politics, leaks, and betrayal inside modern intelligence agencies.

5. The Agency

2024– • Adapted from The Bureau

A modern remake of The Bureau, carrying its psychological depth into a new world.
