All Quiet on the Western Front
2022 • War / Anti-war drama
A teenager enlists full of patriotic excitement, only to be thrown into trench warfare where survival becomes the only goal. The film follows how fear, hunger, and constant shelling erase the idea of “glory” and turn friendship into a lifeline. It’s not a heroic war movie — it’s a suffocating look at what war does to the body and the mind. Best for viewers who want realism and atmosphere over inspiration.
